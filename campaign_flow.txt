AI Campaign Builder Flow
========================
This document outlines the complete 10-step AI Campaign Creation workflow implemented in CreateCampaign.tsx.

Step 1: Goal Definition
-----------------------
- Header: "What's your marketing goal today?"
- Input: Text area for the user to describe their goal (e.g., "I want to bring back customers who purchased before but haven't shopped recently.").
- Helpers: Quick-select "Popular Goals" buttons (Increase repeat purchases, Promote new collection, etc.).
- Action: "Continue" moves to Step 2.

Step 2: AI Strategy Planner
---------------------------
- Header: "AI Strategy Plan"
- Content: The AI recommends a strategy based on the goal.
  - Target Audience: E.g., "Customers inactive > 60 days" with estimated size and revenue.
  - Recommended Offer: E.g., "25% OFF on next purchase".
  - Recommended Channels: Ranked list (WhatsApp, Instagram, Email, Facebook).
- Actions: "Regenerate" or "Approve & Continue" to Step 3.

Step 3: AI Audience Builder
---------------------------
- Header: "Define Your Audience"
- Content: AI generates specific audience criteria (e.g., Haven't purchased in 60 days, Spent > ₹5000, Age 18-35).
- Action: "Find Audience" button triggers a simulated 1.5-second loading state ("Finding...").
- Result: Reveals an "Audience Found" success card showing total customers (2,348) and potential revenue.
- Action: "View Sample" moves to Step 4.

Step 4: AI Audience Insights
----------------------------
- Header: "Audience Insights"
- Content: Data breakdown of the matched audience.
  - Demographics: Gender split (72% Female) and Top Age Group.
  - Top Cities: Percentage breakdown across major cities (Mumbai, Bangalore, etc.).
  - Interest & Behavior: Top product categories and preferred channels.
- Banner: AI insight note ("This audience responded 3x better on WhatsApp...").
- Action: "Continue" moves to Step 5.

Step 5: AI Channel Creator
--------------------------
- Header: "Create Content for Each Channel"
- Content: 
  - Tabs to switch between channels (WhatsApp, Instagram, Email, SMS).
  - Left: Interactive smartphone mockup rendering the WhatsApp message exactly as it would appear on a device.
  - Right: AI insights on "Why this works?" (Personalized greeting, Clear offer) and "Expected Performance" metrics.
- Actions: "Edit Content" or "Next" to Step 6.

Step 6: AI Creative Generator
-----------------------------
- Header: "Creative Generator"
- Content: Visual assets generated by AI.
  - Layout: A 2-column grid. The left side features a dynamic CSS-styled banner overlaying an image ("Summer Collection - 25% OFF").
  - Includes a row of selectable thumbnails for different aspect ratios.
  - The right side features vertical assets (like Instagram Stories).
- Actions: "Regenerate" or "Download All" (which moves to Step 7).

Step 7: AI Personalization
--------------------------
- Header: "Personalization Preview"
- Content: Demonstrates the 1:1 personalization engine.
  - Top: A customer selector dropdown (e.g., Ananya Sharma - Mumbai | Dresses Lover).
  - Bottom: A side-by-side comparison of the generic "Base Template" vs the dynamically "Personalized" copy tailored to the selected customer's data.
- Action: "Continue to Preview" moves to Step 8.

Step 8: Campaign Preview
------------------------
- Header: "Campaign Preview"
- Content: Final review before launch.
  - Left Column: Campaign Summary (Name, Audience Size, Offer, Channels list).
  - Right Column: Content Summary (List of assets generated for each channel with thumbnails and status checkmarks).
- Actions: "Edit Everything" (returns to Step 1) or "Approve & Launch" moves to Step 9.

Step 9: Launch Campaign
-----------------------
- Header: "Your campaign is ready to launch!"
- Content: 
  - Visual: A large purple circle with a Rocket icon surrounded by animated pulsing confetti.
  - Summary Card: Quick recap of the campaign name, audience size, channels, and estimated reach.
- Action: "🚀 Launch Campaign" moves to Step 10.

Step 10: Channel Simulator
--------------------------
- Header: "Channel Service Simulator"
- Content: Simulates the live execution of the campaign.
  - Left Column ("Live Events"): A real-time log of events (e.g., 10:31:02 WhatsApp Delivered, 10:31:05 Instagram Viewed).
  - Right Column ("Channel Status"): Progress bars showing the sending status for each individual channel.
- Action: "View Live Campaign" concludes the flow and navigates the user back to the main Dashboard (simulating the live campaigns tab).
